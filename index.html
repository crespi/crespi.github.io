About

I've been working at the intersection of cryptocurrencies and product design since 2017. Recently, I've worked with companies like DAppNode and Nethermind.

Previously I was Head of Design at Muun, where I helped build and launch a self-custodial Bitcoin wallet for Android and iOS. I hold an MSc. in Cognitive Systems and Interactive Media at Universitat Pompeu Fabra.

In my free time I create abstract 3D animations and play them live as a VJ in parties or live shows.
